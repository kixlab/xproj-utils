Autonomous Region Grant (Administration and Administration Division)
- For the autonomous regions where the amount of the financial income is below the standard financial demand, it is granted based on the financial deficit. - For the special finance demand business of the autonomous region, it is distributed according to the feasibility and financial power.
- Article 173 of the Local Autonomy Act, Article 117 of the Enforcement Decree of the Local Autonomy Act, and the Ordinance on the Financial Coordination of the Seoul Metropolitan Government Borough
- Jan. ~ Feb.: Establishment of monthly grants for ordinary grants - Jan. ~ Dec.: Issuance of ordinary grants and special grants for autonomous districts - 5 ~ Aug.: Calculation - September - November: Normal grants allocation calculation
