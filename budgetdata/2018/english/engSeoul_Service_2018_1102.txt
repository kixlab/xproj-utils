
Citizen Health Bureau health care policy section
Health
Improve management and operation of public medical institutions
○ Establish exemplary public healthcare system and strengthen its function. ○ Provide high-quality medical services to citizens at low cost by increasing the rate of hospitalization while maintaining public medical functions. ○ Effective use of the medical resources that have been put in place, Increase efficiency
○ Support basis: Article 9 (2) of the Ordinance on the Establishment and Operation of Seoul Metropolitan Hospital
○ Proceeding: Concluded with the Ewha Womans University (May 2007)
