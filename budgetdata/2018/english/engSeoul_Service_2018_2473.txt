
Women's Policy Office, Women Policy Officer
Social Welfare
Internal Trade Expenditure (Women Policy Office, Women Policy Officer)
○ Period: 2016. ~ 2016. 12. ○ ○ Business contents: Women's Development Fund public offering project
○ Rationale for the Act: Article 142 of the 「Local Autonomy Act」, Articles 42 and 43 of the 「Gender Equality Basic Act」, Articles 38 and 39 of the 「Gender Equality Basic Act of Seoul Metropolitan Government」
○ Established in 1996 to facilitate the promotion of gender equality, including promotion of women's development policies. Starting from 1998, 1,065 organizations, 1,172 projects, and 14,333 million won were supported from 1998 to 2015.
