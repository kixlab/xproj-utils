
Disaster Response Division, Fire and Disaster Headquarters
Public order and safety
Installation and management of fire fighting water facilities
Maintenance of fire-fighting water facilities, painting and complementary work, maintenance of fire-fighting manhole, maintenance of emergency fire extinguishing equipment, etc.
- Article 10 of the Framework Act on Firefighting (Installation and management of fire fighting water facilities, etc.)
- Enforcement Rule of the Basic Law for Fire Service Article 6 (Standards for the installation of fire fighting water facilities)
• When installing in residential, commercial or industrial areas - Horizontal distance less than 100 meters
• For installation in other areas - Horizontal distance less than 140 meters
