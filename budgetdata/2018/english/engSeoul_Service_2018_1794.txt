Mental health facility operation
○ Operation of social rehabilitation facilities to promote the return to society of chronic mentally ill persons
○ Support basis: Articles 15 and 52 of the Mental Health Act
○ Proceeding in 1997: Establishment of a mental health rehabilitation facility with the enactment of the Mental Health Law Enforcement Rule - '04: According to 'Mental Health 2020' plan (Apr. 04, 2003, Market Policy No. 600) Establishment of infrastructure for mental health facilities in earnest - '06 ~ '07: Installation and operation of 4 residential facilities supported by hospitals
