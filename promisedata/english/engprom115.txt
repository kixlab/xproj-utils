○ Establishment of 'Classical Concert Hall' which is one of the best in the world
Create a music city
- Performances through the fostering of world-class orchestras, creating demand for tourism culture
- Function as a mecca of classical music where various artists and musicians of the world meet and communicate with each other
 Promotion plan (policy outline)
○ Necessity of erection: Absolute lack of dedicated performance facility for demand of classical building
- Concert hall of Seoul Arts Center, Seoul's only exclusive class facility Annual facility utilization rate exceeded 100%
○ Promotion Direction
- Establishment of 'authentic concert hall' where Seoul can become the center city of world classical music
- Establishment of foundation for all citizens to enjoy high class culture without class
○ Promotion contents
- Selection of the best site to create synergy of cultural enjoyment and utilization of tourism resources
- Establishment of authentic concert hall optimized for classical performances
· Design and facilities equipped with 'sound' centered venue
- Build the best facility for musicians and domestic and foreign tourists to use conveniently
· Performance room
○ Project Period: 2014.7 ~ 2023
